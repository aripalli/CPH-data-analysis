Nationality in Copenhagen over the years

In this section we explore the different nationalities in Copenhagen and how they have developed over the years. The bar chart shows for a given year the top 25 nationalties based on number of people. Using the slider makes it possible to look at different years and how things have changed. It can for instance be seen how it is only in recent years that the Swedes took the first place from the Turks. The graph below shows how the number of people from a given nation has changed over the years. It is also possible to look at different districts, and also to look at the distribution of danes vs. foreigners. Clicking on a bar in the bar chart takes you to that given nation. Happy exploring!
